About the Libary

The Library Media Center consists of three floors with each one dedicated to one media access. The first floor houses the books, information desk, check-out counter, video collection, and the student study areas. The second floor houses the computer lab. The third floor houses the video production lab, sound production lab, and the technology research lab.

Procedures

Student Expectations

  • Students may use the AHSLMC before and after school.
  • Any disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Students must also sign-in and sign-out at the checkout desk.
  • Students are expected to behave in a productive manner.

    • Borrowing Privileges

  • Student ID’s are required to check out all AHSLMC Materials.
  • Books are checked out for two weeks and may be renewed.
  • Reference materials circulate for two days.
  • Calculators may be checked out overnight.

    • Internet of Use

  • The use of computers is limited to one hour.
  • Student Internet use is to be assignment related
  • E-mail is allowed for homework purposes only.
  • Chatting, instant messaging, and downloading is forbidden.

    • Miscellaneous

  • Food and beverages of any kind are prohibited.
  • Backpacks, gym bags, book bags are prohibited.
  • Students may bring headphones to work on material.
  • Personal electronic devices are allow in the AHSLMC.
